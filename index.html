At the time of its release, the W1 was Japan’s largest-displacement motorcycle, and boasted top-level horsepower. Designed to be the fastest, most powerful machines of their day, Kawasaki’s W Series models are now better known for a beauty that never goes out of style, and the rich character of an air-cooled engine. To this day, Japan’s original big bikes continue to command a respect . To ride a W is to feel the pride of being connected to such illustrious roots.
